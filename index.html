"Father of The Web" Tim Berners-Lee

"The Father of HTML"

  • HTML is the standard markup language for creating web pages.
  • Tim Berners-Lee "The Father of HTML" is the man who wrote the first version of HTML(HyperText Markup Language) in 1993.
  • Tim was a contractor at the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN) back in the 80's and the 90's when he developed HTML.
  • Over 20 years later there have been many different versions of HTML but the language itself is still the basis of the world's 4.2 billion plus webpages.
